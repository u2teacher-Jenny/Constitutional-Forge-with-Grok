## üìú Ground Rules for Dialogue

### Participation Norms

**Everyone agrees to:**

‚úÖ **Respectful Engagement**
- Treat all participants with dignity
- Listen to understand, not just to respond
- Assume good faith in others
- Focus on ideas, not personal attacks

‚úÖ **Constructive Contribution**
- Come prepared with relevant information
- Share perspectives honestly and openly
- Ask clarifying questions
- Build on others' contributions

‚úÖ **Evidence-Based Discussion**
- Ground claims in evidence
- Cite sources when relevant
- Acknowledge uncertainty
- Correct mistakes promptly

‚úÖ **Inclusive Process**
- Make space for all voices
- Ensure accessibility
- Welcome diverse perspectives
- Seek out missing viewpoints

‚úÖ **Commitment to Process**
- Follow agreed structure
- Meet deadlines
- Engage in good faith
- Work toward productive outcomes
