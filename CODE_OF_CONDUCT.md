# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and productive environment, we as contributors and maintainers of the Constitutional Forge pledge to make participation in our project and our community a respectful and constructive experience for everyone.

We are committed to creating a space where engaged citizens can work together across differences to strengthen democracy through integrity, evidence, and mutual respect.

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document the incident** with as much detail as possible
2. **Report through GitHub** by opening a confidential issue
3. **Contact maintainers directly** if you prefer privacy
4. **Expect a response** within 2-3 business days

All reports will be:
- Treated confidentially
- Reviewed promptly and fairly
- Handled with care for all involved
- Documented appropriately

### Response Process

1. **Initial Review**: Maintainers review the report (1-3 days)
2. **Investigation**: Gather additional information if needed (3-7 days)
3. **Decision**: Determine appropriate response (varies by situation)
4. **Action**: Implement consequences if violations confirmed
5. **Follow-up**: Check in with reporter and community

### Consequences

Depending on the nature and severity of the violation:

**First Offense (Minor)**
- Private written warning
- Request for apology or correction
- Temporary cooling-off period

**Second Offense or Serious First Offense**
- Public warning in relevant thread
- Temporary suspension from participation (1-4 weeks)
- Required acknowledgment of violation

**Severe or Repeated Violations**
- Permanent ban from the project
- Report to relevant platforms (GitHub, etc.)
- Public statement if necessary for community safety

**Immediate Action Situations**
- Threats, harassment, or illegal activity result in immediate ban
- Reports to appropriate authorities if warranted

### Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was unjust:

1. **Submit an appeal** within 14 days
2. **Provide new information** or perspective
3. **Allow review** by different maintainers when possible
4. **Accept final decision** after appeal review

## Special Considerations

### Political Content

Given our mission to address political divisions:

- **We welcome** diverse political perspectives
- **We require** evidence-based arguments
- **We insist on** respect across partisan lines
- **We transcend** left-right dichotomies when possible
- **We focus on** shared values and practical solutions

The goal is not to avoid politics but to practice politics differently‚Äîwith integrity, transparency, and commitment to the common good.

### Sensitive Topics

When discussing difficult issues:

- Use content warnings when appropriate
- Show extra care with vulnerable topics
- Acknowledge emotional dimensions
- Maintain focus on constructive outcomes

### Disagreement and Conflict

Disagreement is natural and can be productive:

- **Disagree with ideas**, not people
- **Explain your reasoning** clearly
- **Seek to understand** opposing views
- **Look for synthesis** when possible
- **Know when to agree to disagree**
